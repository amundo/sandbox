        View the rest of the documentation on keyboard shortcuts here.

        • a
          Inserts the assigned character when you press the a key.
        • \
          Inserts the assigned character when you press the \ key.
        • ?
          Inserts the assigned character when you press Shift+/ together.
        • ctrl+a
          Inserts the assigned character when you press Ctrl+a together.
        • ctrl+A
          Inserts the assigned character when you press Ctrl+Shift + A together.
        • a =
          Inserts the assigned character when you press a and = in sequence.
        • a @ 1
          Inserts the assigned character when you press a, Shift+2, 1, and 1 in sequence.
        • ctrl+/ a
          Inserts the assigned character when you press Ctrl+/, then a in sequence
        • a b c =
          Inserts the assigned character when you press a, b, c, and = in sequence.
